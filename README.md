# Overview

Access to quality healthcare remains uneven across India. This project explores healthcare infrastructure distribution, healthcare demand, and supply gaps across different states and cities using interactive visualizations.

The dashboard helps identify underserved regions, healthcare concentration patterns, and infrastructure inequalities to support data-driven decision-making.

---

# Problem Statement

Healthcare resources are not distributed equally across India. Some metropolitan regions possess a high concentration of healthcare facilities while several regions continue to face shortages.

This project aims to:

- Analyze healthcare availability across regions.
- Compare healthcare supply and demand.
- Identify infrastructure gaps.
- Highlight underserved areas requiring attention.
- Support policy and planning decisions through data visualization.

# Key Insights

### 1. Extreme Inequality
Healthcare services are highly concentrated in a few urban regions.

### 2. Urban Dominance
Major metropolitan cities such as Hyderabad, Mumbai, and Bangalore dominate healthcare availability.

### 3. Under-served Regions
Several regions show low healthcare infrastructure despite significant population demand.

### 4. Supply-Demand Gap
Certain areas experience substantial mismatches between healthcare demand and available resources.

### 5. Infrastructure Concentration
Healthcare facilities are clustered around urban centers, creating accessibility challenges for rural populations.

### 6. Regional Disparities
Healthcare development varies significantly across different states and regions.

# Results & Conclusion

The analysis reveals significant healthcare infrastructure disparities across India.

Key findings include:

- Strong concentration of healthcare services in metropolitan areas.
- Several underserved regions with limited healthcare accessibility.
- Noticeable supply-demand imbalances.
- Opportunities for improved healthcare planning and resource allocation.

The dashboard demonstrates how data visualization can help identify critical healthcare gaps and support informed decision-making.
